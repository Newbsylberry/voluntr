Thanks for your interest in registering for Volu, you are a few clicks away from having the ability to manage the opportunities your organization provides, and the people interested in them. Before we move forward, we want you to know exactly what data we're accessing from your account, and why.

Information About Your Organization

When you register for Volu, we save your organizations name, a brief description, a link to your current Facebook picture, and the identification number that Facebook uses. This is so volunteers who don't have a Facebook account can find out about your organization, and so we can ensure that anyone who tries to access your Volu account has permission.

Your Organization's Facebook Page Posts

At Volu, we believe that connecting with your community begins long before someone actually comes in contact with your organization. We import your Facebook posts, and insights about them, so that we can help you get a better understanding of what has helped you connect with your audience in the past, and how to build off your success.

The People Who Like Those Posts

Liking a post on your Facebook wall is the first step to getting involved and supporting your organizations mission. When you register for Volu, we want to see you succeed, so we give you a list of people you have already impacted through your marketing.

Oh yeah, and all of the above we do for free, so what are you waiting for?

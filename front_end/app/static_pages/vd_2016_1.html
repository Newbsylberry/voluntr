Introduction

The VOLUnteer Drive is a month-long civic engagement challenge where volunteer groups in Onondaga County will compete to record the most hours at local nonprofits. Volu, a volunteer management software company based in Syracuse, will be providing the technology required to execute the VOLUnteer Drive and offering up to $5,000 in micro-grants to local nonprofit organizations to host volunteer events. Additional information can be found by reading our request for proposals.

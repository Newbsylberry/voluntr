Volu Is For Your Community

Volu is an application intended to help non-profit organizations reach their volunteers and future leaders. Non-profit organizations can manage their events and social engagement through facebook, and individuals can see what volunteer events and opportunities are available to them.

  • Manage Your Current Volunteers
  • Identify Your Future Volunteers
  • Manage All Your Social Platforms

With Volu, managing the people who support your organization is easy. Importing their information from an existing database, or through Facebook, are just a couple of options your organization has available.

Through advanced analytics, Volu identifies which volunteers have provided the most value to your organization, and finds similar individuals to help support your mission.

Volu gives you access to all your likes, retweets, mentions, and any other social network information that you might find useful down the road.

Find nearby volunteering opportunities that match your interests, set goals, and organize your team to compete for bragging rights.

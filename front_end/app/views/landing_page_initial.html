VOLU is a non-profit, open source, mobile and desktop platform that enables non-profit organizations to easily reach volunteers and plan smarter events. Getting started is easy, and due to its open source nature, the possibilities of VOLU are endless.

For Organizations:

Log in with a Facebook account that has administrator privileges to your organizations Facebook page and start adding events you need volunteers for. Your personal Facebook information will not be stored.

For Volunteers:

Sign up for a VOLU account and see what volunteer events are happening close by, record your volunteer hours, organize teams, compete against friends, and see the effect your volunteering has on the community.
